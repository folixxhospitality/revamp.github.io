Our Story

FoLiXx Hospitality, established in 2018, is a thriving hospitality brand in Nigeria

At FoLiXx Hospitality, we are redefining the essence of entertainment and hospitality by crafting vibrant environments where guests can relax, indulge, and create lasting memories. Our foundation rests on core values of innovation, authenticity, diversity, and excellence, bringing together a collection of unique brands tailored to a wide range of tastes and lifestyles.

From the lively atmosphere of our quick-service restaurant, FoLiXx Bukka, to the refined dining experience at our mid-scale restaurant, The Glass House, where culinary mastery meets diverse palates, to the upscale allure of Secrets Palace, our premier nightclub offering discreet and sophisticated adult entertainment, we cater to a spectrum of desires with precision and flair.

At FoLiXx Hospitality, we are driven by our commitment to deliver impeccable service and unrivaled sophistication across all of our venues. More than just a brand, FoLiXx Hospitality is a lifestyle deeply connected to our community. Each of our spaces is thoughtfully designed to offer a unique experience, ensuring that whether you're enjoying a meal, dancing the night away, or savoring a night out, your time with us is nothing short of extraordinary. We promise an experience so remarkable, you'll be eager to return. Our dedication to excellence extends beyond our venues to our staff, who are carefully selected and trained to provide the highest level of service and attention to detail.
